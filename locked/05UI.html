Project 00

Unemployment Insurance

To demostrate a systematic and centralized approach of the Unemployment Insurance (UI) modernization

  • Project | Date on the project Unemployment Insurance State Pilot | 2021 - 2022
  • Client | Partner U.S. Department of Labor | U.S. Digital Services
  • Role Design Lead

The problem 

While some states struggled to adjust their intake and backend processing systems, other states seized the opportunity to build new architectures to sit alongside the old, and still others attempted hybrid solutions. While every state responded to the best of their technical ability, the inefficiency and overall cost of 53 agencies scrambling to respond individually to the same Federal guidance highlighed the need to think more deeply about a centralized solution for all or part of the UI lifecycle.

UI systems were ill-equipped to respond quickly to UI claimant surges due to COVID-19 and the resulting Federal benefit programs

 

The proposal

The proposal is a systematic approach to a centralized effort and digital modernization i response to ARPA - the American Rescue Plan Act. The pilot begins with the Claimant experience across the Unemployment Insurance.
