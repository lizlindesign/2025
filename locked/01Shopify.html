The challenge for the mobile widget was to provide merchants with the most important BFCM sales stats in a tiny space without overwhelming them, since "a wealth of information can create a poverty of attention". To address this, dozens of variations were create and tested to find the best layout and visual representation of real-time sales statistics. Through this extensive exploration process, we were able to identify the most effective and the best design for the mobile widget.
